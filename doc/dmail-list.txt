usage: dmail list [-c <count>| --count=<count>] [-u | --unread] [--mailbox=<mailbox>]

Lists emails.

    -c <count>, --count=<count>     Limit the number of emails fetched. Default: 10
    -u, --unread                    Fetches only unread emails
    --mailbox=<mailbox>             Specifies which mailbox to read from. Default: INBOX
